Revenge Of The Knight

The year is 1431. It all began with a knight living happily in a small village along with other knights and their families. One night the ground began to shake, Hades (the god of the underworld) rose out of the underworld and attacked the village out of nowhere because he was bored. All hell broke loose, death after death happened. Hades burned the whole village down. Fire everywhere, wooden houses burning down until nothing was left over.
